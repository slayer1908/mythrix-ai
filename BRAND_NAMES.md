# Brand Name Shortlist — APEX.AI Rebrand

> ## ✅ DECISION (May 2026): **MYTHRIX.AI**
> The codebase has been renamed from APEX.AI to MYTHRIX.AI throughout — package name, class names, color tokens, storage keys, URLs, env vars, logo wordmark, and all docs. This file remains as a record of the naming process.

---


A market scan of the AI-marketing space across 20+ candidate names, with the verified shortlist that is **not currently used by an AI, marketing, or SaaS company** as of May 2026.

---

## Why "APEX" needs to change

"Apex" is heavily used in tech and marketing. The web scan turned up multiple existing brands using "Apex" in adjacent spaces (Apex Marketing, Apex consulting, Apex Tools). Trademark conflict is likely on a `.com` / `.ai` registration.

## How the shortlist was built

1. Surveyed the AI-marketing competitive set — Syntermedia, Albert.ai, Madgicx, Marin, Omneky, Smartly, Tofu, NoimosAI, AthenaHQ, RankAI, ZyG, HubSpot, Marketo, Iterable, Klaviyo, Jasper, Copy.ai, MarketMuse, Optmyzr, Creatio, Brandforge.
2. Brainstormed 30+ candidates across naming patterns: invented brandables, Latin roots, compound modern words, mythological references.
3. Web-searched each candidate individually for direct collisions or close phonetic matches.

## ✅ Verified Shortlist — 5 names with no current AI/marketing collision

### 1. Outlume *(top pick)*
- **Pronunciation:** OUT-loom
- **Meaning:** *Out + lumen* — to "outshine" the rest. The brand outputs light into your marketing.
- **Vibe:** Confident, premium, slightly literary. Lands close to "Lumen" without colliding.
- **Strengths:** Six letters, no existing tech brand. Suggests illumination + outperforming.
- **Tagline direction:** *"Outshine every channel."*
- **Status:** No exact-match company or trademark found.

### 2. Markeon
- **Pronunciation:** MAR-kee-on
- **Meaning:** *Market + eon* — the new era of marketing.
- **Vibe:** Bold, modern, slightly futuristic. Sounds like a Marvel-grade brand.
- **Strengths:** Self-explanatory to anyone in marketing. Strong tonal match to "autonomous AI for marketing."
- **Tagline direction:** *"A new era of marketing."*
- **Status:** No exact-match AI/marketing company found.

### 3. Mythrix
- **Pronunciation:** MITH-rix
- **Meaning:** *Myth + matrix* — legend-grade automation that becomes the stuff of marketing folklore.
- **Vibe:** Bold, premium, slightly mystical. Excellent for an "agentic" AI brand.
- **Strengths:** Memorable. Mythic-AI is in chips, Mythik is in entertainment — neither overlaps with marketing SaaS.
- **Tagline direction:** *"Marketing, mythologized."*
- **Status:** No exact-match in AI marketing. Adjacent brands (Mythic, Mythik) operate in different sectors.

### 4. Brennox
- **Pronunciation:** BREN-ox
- **Meaning:** Invented. Latinate feel — evokes brennen (to burn, German) + nox (night, Latin). Power working through the night.
- **Vibe:** Sharp, distinctive, slightly noir. Fits a "campaigns running 24/7" narrative.
- **Strengths:** Truly unique — no AI, marketing, or SaaS company by this name. Brandix/Branex are different enough not to compete.
- **Tagline direction:** *"Burn brighter. Spend smarter."*
- **Status:** Clear.

### 5. Praevia
- **Pronunciation:** PRAY-vee-uh
- **Meaning:** Latin "ahead of," "going before." Suggests a system that runs ahead of you — predicting, optimizing, acting first.
- **Vibe:** Sophisticated, European, premium-enterprise feel. Lands like a McKinsey-of-AI brand.
- **Strengths:** No conflict with any AI marketing brand. Strong meaning aligned with "autonomous" / "always-on."
- **Tagline direction:** *"Always one step ahead."*
- **Status:** Clear.

## Before you commit — DO THIS

1. **Domain check** (5 min): Visit Namecheap or GoDaddy and confirm `.ai`, `.com`, `.io` and the founder's email address (`hello@…`) are available or buyable. Priority: `.ai`.
2. **Trademark check** (10 min): Search [USPTO TESS](https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/) for the exact word and any phonetically similar one. Class 9 (software) and Class 42 (SaaS) are the ones that matter.
3. **Social handles** (5 min): Check the handle is free or claimable on X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, GitHub.
4. **App Store name** (5 min): Search Apple App Store + Google Play. App store names are first-come-first-served.
5. **Say it out loud** (1 min): Phone yourself with the name 5 times. Does it land? Can you spell it without spelling it out?
